3 Milnor fibers

Milnor fiber is the fiber over 1 of the Milnor fibration of the hyperplane arrangement. The following describes functions to construct regular cell complex having the homotopy type of the Milnor fiber and thus enables computation of homotopy invaraints using e.g. the HAP package.
